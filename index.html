Stuffed Rigatoni Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Water and salt
  • Rigatoni
  • Olive oil
  • Ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp parsley
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup mozzarella
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup bread crumbs

Steps:

  1. Boil the rigatoni pasta with water and salt until done, then mix them with some olive oil and let them cool.
  2. Mix ground beef, salt, oregano, parsley, onion, garlic, and mozzarella together.
  3. Put the mixture in a freezer bag and squeeze it into the rigatoni.
  4. Freeze the rigatoni for 30 minutes.
  5. Coat the rigatoni with flour, eggs, and bread crumbs.
  6. Fry them with oil until golden brown.
  7. Enjoy your delicious stuffed rigatoni!
Gnocchi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 potatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 1-1 1/2 cups flour
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Boil the potatoes, peel them, then smash them. Add salt, pepper, and one egg, and mix them.
  2. Take the dough out of the bowl and add the flour, then knead it until it forms a dough.
  3. Shape the dough into desired shapes and boil them until they float.
  4. Fry them with butter, and they're done.
Caesar Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves (crushed)
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • French baguette (1 or any other bread)
  • 1/2 head iceberg lettuce
  • Salt to taste
  • 1-2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp thyme

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil and butter in a pan and cook the garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken breast and cook until fully cooked, then set aside and slice.
  3. Toast the French baguette (or any bread) in the pan, then cut it into crouton-sized pieces.
  4. Mix the mayonnaise, parmesan cheese, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper in a bowl to make the dressing.
  5. Take the iceberg lettuce and tear it into bite-sized pieces, then place it in a bowl.
  6. Add the chicken, croutons, and dressing to the lettuce, and toss everything together.
  7. Enjoy your Caesar salad!
Garlic Parmesan Potato Wedges Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves (crushed)
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • French baguette (1 or any other bread)
  • 1/2 head iceberg lettuce
  • Salt to taste
  • 1-2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp thyme

Steps:

  1. Cut the potatoes into wedges and mix them with olive oil.
  2. Season with your choice of spices.
  3. Bake at 400°F / 200°C for 40 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Enjoy your garlic parmesan potato wedges!
Mandazi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 600g self-rising flour (or 600g all-purpose flour + 6 tsp baking powder)
  • 6 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp soft butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1-2 tbsp cinnamon powder

Steps:

  1. Mix the flour, cinnamon, margarine (or butter), and sugar in a suitable bowl. Make sure the margarine is broken up into the flour.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs into the milk. Make a well at the center of the flour and add the milk and egg mixture. Slowly mix to form a dough.
  3. Knead the dough for 3-4 minutes or until it stops sticking to the sides of the bowl and you have a smooth surface.
  4. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and allow it to rest for 15 minutes.
  5. Roll the dough on a lightly floured surface into a 1cm thick piece.
  6. Using a sharp knife, cut the dough into the desired size, setting it aside ready for deep frying.
  7. Heat oil in a suitable pot and gently dip the mandazi pieces to cook until light brown on the first side, then turn to cook on the second side.
  8. Serve them warm or cold.
